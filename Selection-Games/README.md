# Learn2Earn.ng Selection Game Description

The Learn2Earn.ng selection game is an innovative online assessment designed to identify candidates with strong problem-solving abilities and potential for technical training. This game serves as the initial gateway for applicants seeking admission into the Learn2Earn program, which offers free education in exchange for future work commitments.

## Key Features of the Selection Game

### **Purpose and Structure**
The selection game is crafted as a fun, engaging challenge that tests fundamental skills including:
- Logical reasoning
- Memory retention
- Problem-solving capabilities
- Critical thinking abilities

The game serves as an accessible entry point for individuals without prior technical backgrounds to demonstrate their aptitude for learning technology concepts.

### **Game Duration and Flexibility**
The selection process typically takes approximately 90 minutes to complete. During the game, players must maintain continuous engagement and cannot take breaks. The platform features a visible countdown timer that tracks the remaining time. Additionally, players are required to reach a minimum performance level or score threshold to be considered for advancement in the selection process.


### **Role in the Selection Process**
The selection game is the first step in a multi-stage evaluation process:
1. Online game completion
2. Invitation to Trials (30-day in-person coding bootcamp)
3. Final selection for full program admission

This sequential approach ensures that only candidates demonstrating sufficient potential advance to more intensive phases of evaluation.

### **Accessibility and Approachability**
The design philosophy emphasizes accessibility, requiring no prior technical knowledge or experience. Instead, the game focuses on:
- Natural learning ability
- Curiosity and engagement
- Capacity to absorb new concepts quickly
- Problem-solving mindset

This approach democratizes access to technology education by focusing on potential rather than existing skill levels.

### **Preparation and Experience**
Candidates can approach the selection game with confidence knowing it's designed to be:
- Intuitive and user-friendly
- Free to access
- Representative of real-world problem-solving scenarios
- A genuine assessment of capability rather than rote memorization

The game provides a fair opportunity for all participants to showcase their analytical skills and learning potential, regardless of their educational background or prior experience in technology fields.

This selection mechanism represents Learn2Earn's innovative approach to identifying talent while maintaining accessibility for diverse candidates seeking technical education opportunities.
