# Learn2Earn Nigeria (Learn2Earn.ng)

[![Platform](https://img.shields.io/badge/Platform-Web%20%7C%20Onsite-blueviolet?style=for-the-badge)](https://learn2earn.ng) [![License](https://img.shields.io/badge/License-MIT-green?style=for-the-badge)](LICENSE) [![Focus](https://img.shields.io/badge/Focus-AI%20%26%20FullStack%20Engineering-orange?style=for-the-badge)](https://learn2earn.ng)

Learn2Earn.ng is a zero-tuition, peer-to-peer training ecosystem that converts raw problem-solving talent into industry-ready AI and full-stack engineers. Fellows are selected via a gamified logic challenge, trained through immersive peer-driven programs, and transitioned into guaranteed paid placements.

## Vision & Mission

Vision: position Nigeria (and Africa) as a global hub for AI-native and full-stack engineering talent by removing financial and credential barriers.

Mission: discover high-potential individuals, train them through intensive peer-to-peer learning, and deploy them into guaranteed paid roles—without upfront tuition.

---

## Problem Statement

- High tuition and paywalled bootcamps exclude many high-potential candidates.
- Employers require experience that early-career candidates can't obtain (the experience paradox).
- Traditional curricula are often outdated for AI and modern full-stack demands.

---

## Why Learn2Earn

- 100% scholarship model: no upfront fees for selection, training, or mentorship.
- Meritocratic selection via a logic-based game rather than resumes.
- Peer-to-peer, project-driven learning that emphasizes collaboration and real deliverables.
- Guaranteed 2-year paid placement upon successful graduation.

---

## Who Should Apply

- Unemployed or underemployed graduates and school leavers.
- Self-taught learners who need real-world, collaborative experience.
- Analytical individuals (math, sciences, chess, etc.) with strong problem-solving potential.
- No prior coding experience required.

---

## Program Flow

1. Logic Challenge (1.5–2 hours): browser game assessing reasoning and persistence.
2. Piscine (4 weeks onsite): immersive peer-driven bootcamp focused on learning-by-doing.
3. Core Fellowship (multi-month): specialized technical tracks, team projects, and deployment readiness.
4. Deployment (2-year contract): guaranteed paid placement with employer partners.

## Getting Started (Developers & Fellows)

Developers: clone the core repo, set environment variables, and run services locally via Docker.

Example:

```bash
git clone https://github.com/Learn2EarnNG/core-ecosystem.git
cd core-ecosystem
cp .env.example .env
docker-compose up -d --build
make init-env
```

Fellows: visit https://learn2earn.ng to take the logic challenge when applications are open.

## FAQ

Q: Is the program free?
A: Yes — selection, training, and mentorship are free. Costs are recovered through outcome-aligned partnerships after deployment.

Q: Do I need prior coding experience?
A: No. The entrance game evaluates logic and persistence, not syntax knowledge.

Q: How long is the commitment after placement?
A: Fellows typically sign a 2-year employment contract with partner organizations.
